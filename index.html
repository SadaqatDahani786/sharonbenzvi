    About

    Ben-zvi real1 deals with locating land, property marketing, strategic consulting and real estate relations for companies and entrepreneurs

    Sharon Ben Zvi who founded the company is connected to the industry and has been involved in the practice of the real estate world for nearly three decades

    Served for many years as the VP of Marketing and Sales in one of the largest and most stable listed companies in the market, managed, initiated and was a partner in leading huge transactions. With her knowledge, experience, business connections and her love for the profession, she established the company and continues to establish its activities through it even today

    Sharon serves as a director, a lecturer at the academy, a social activist and brings uncompromising professionalism, reliability and meaning to the company

    "We are what we build"

    Sharon began her journey in the worlds of real estate in 1995,

    In 1999 she joined the Levinstein Group and for 20 years served as VP of Marketing and Sales. There she filled her backpack with a lot of knowledge and sharpening her business and interpersonal values.

    As VP, she was a partner in the whole of the strategy processes, the identification of opportunities in developing areas, the planning of the projects and their marketing. Was responsible for branding, positioning, raising and maintaining the group's reputation.

    In the residential area , she was involved in establishing and managing the marketing division, recruiting and training sales teams, planning the projects and establishing all the marketing infrastructures that led to the sale of thousands of housing units across the country.

    In the field of yields, it led to the leasing of hundreds of thousands of meters of office and trading in the towers built by the group with optimal returns, and to the closing of huge deals with leading companies such as Google, Playtica, Ironsource, Similar Web, bank embassies, law firms and more.

    In 2019 she joined the Ofer Investments group as VP of Marketing and in 2022 she gathered all her knowledge, experience and love for the profession and all her business connections and founded the parent company B Real - Sharon Ben Zvi Real Estate Marketing and Real Estate Ltd, of which Ben Zvi Real is one the company in which.
